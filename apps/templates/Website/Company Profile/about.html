ABOUT US

Welcome to Ethio Biamer Trading PLC, your trusted partner in the import and export sector. Since our inception, we have been dedicated to providing high-quality products and services that cater to the diverse needs of our customers. With a strong commitment to excellence, we have established ourselves as a leading trading company in Ethiopia, fostering both local and international partnerships.

OUR JOURNEY

Ethio Biamer Trading PLC was founded with a clear vision: to bridge local markets with quality products and services that enhance everyday life. Our journey began with a focus on importing essential goods, and over the years, we have expanded our operations to include a wide range of products and services. Our growth has been fueled by our unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction, innovation, and ethical business practices.

WHAT WE DO

IMPORTS

We specialize in importing a variety of high-demand goods that are crucial to the Ethiopian market. Our offerings include reliable vehicles, heavy-duty trucks, high-quality edible oils, durable iron bars, sanitary fixtures, and tires. Each product is sourced from reputable manufacturers, ensuring that our customers receive only the best.

EXPORTS

Our commitment to quality is also reflected in our export operations. We proudly export premium Ethiopian coffee, a product renowned for its unique flavor and rich aroma. By supporting local farmers and promoting Ethiopian coffee on the global stage, we contribute to the growth of the agricultural sector and the local economy.

ADDITIONAL SERVICES

Beyond trading, Ethio Biamer Trading PLC offers a range of additional services designed to meet the needs of our clients. Our machine rental services provide businesses with access to the equipment they need without the burden of ownership. In addition, our real estate services focus on developing sustainable living and working spaces that enhance community well-being.

THE GOLDEN BEAN EXPORT EXCELLENCE AWARD

We are thrilled to announce that Roha Coffee has been honored with the prestigious Golden Bean Export Excellence Award! This accolade is a testament to our unwavering commitment to quality, sustainability, and innovation in the coffee export industry.

At Roha Coffee, we take immense pride in delivering the finest Ethiopian coffee beans to the global market. This award recognizes our dedication to ensuring that every cup brewed from our beans tells a story of rich heritage and exceptional craftsmanship.
