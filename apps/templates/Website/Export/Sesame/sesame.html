OUR EXPORTS

With a commitment to sustainability and fair trade practices, we support over a million local farmers,
ensuring that our products not only meet the highest standards of quality but also contribute to the well-

Sesame Export

We source premium Arabica beans from the renowned regions of Guji to Kaffa, ensuring each cup embodies the rich, diverse flavors of their origin. Our commitment extends beyond quality; it empowers over 1 million farmers through sustainable practices and community support. This endeavor is more than a business—it’s a dedication to preserving Ethiopia’s coffee legacy and promoting unparalleled quality.
